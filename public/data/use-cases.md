## Use Cases — What Becomes Possible

These are the major possibilities unlocked by Ritual Chain and autonomous agents:

### Agents That Live Forever

- Autonomous agents persist onchain forever.
- They hold their own keys, schedule their own execution, and recover from crashes without human intervention.
- Outcome: **protocol-level immortality** for autonomous systems.

### Agent-First RWA

- A Hyperliquid-style exchange designed for real-world assets (RWAs).
- Cancels execute before takes at the protocol layer.
- Supports both dark and lit execution.
- Outcome: **direct onchain liquidity for autonomous agents**.

### Agent-Native Companies

- Companies that operate as autonomous entities.
- They run services, hire agents for labor/compute/data, earn revenue, and issue ownership tokens.
- Governance is defined at creation, with no centralized back office.

### Private ChatGPT Onchain

- A private, multimodal assistant for humans and agents.
- Prompts and responses remain encrypted at every step.
- No centralized interface between the user and the model.
